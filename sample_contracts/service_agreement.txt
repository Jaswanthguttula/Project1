This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2025 ("Effective Date")
between TechCorp Inc. ("Provider") and ClientCo LLC ("Client").

The Provider shall provide software development services as described in Exhibit A. The Provider

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of twelve

3.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party

3.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination, Client shall pay all outstanding fees for services rendered up to the
termination date.

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary and
confidential information. Confidential information shall not be disclosed to third parties
without prior written consent.

or independently developed.

with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
